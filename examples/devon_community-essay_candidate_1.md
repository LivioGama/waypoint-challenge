# Tomorrow's Lesson — Devon (Grade 7)

## Part A — Teacher Cheat Sheet

### Before the bell
- [[NEW]]Put Devon’s modified packet on his desk before students enter: the 1-page reading organizer, the 4 MCQs, and the short-response page with sentence stems.[[/NEW]]
- [[NEW]]Have a sticky note ready with the day’s co-regulation script and the red-card reminder: “You can use your card anytime. I’ll nod and keep teaching.”[[/NEW]]
- [[NEW]]Pre-mark where Devon should stop after each chunk: after Paragraphs 1–2, after Paragraphs 3–7, and after the Independent Practice MCQs #1–4.[[/NEW]]

### Watch for
- [[NEW]]Stomach hold, staring at the page, or shutting the notebook right when the text moves into the bullet lists in Paragraph 2. That’s a common “too much at once” moment for him.[[/NEW]]
- [[NEW]]A long pause, paper crumpling, or going blank during the MCQs — especially #1 and #4, which ask for the main idea and how paragraphs work together.[[/NEW]]
- [[NEW]]Rubbing his face, asking to leave, or pushing the packet away when the short response starts. That’s usually performance anxiety, not defiance.[[/NEW]]

### If they disengage
1. [[NEW]]Quietly point to the red card or the warning-signs sheet and say: “You can use your break or we can do the next 2 lines together.”[[/NEW]]
2. [[NEW]]After the break, give him just the next small piece: “Read Paragraph 3 or answer MCQ #1 only. I’ll check back in one minute.”[[/NEW]]

### Praise specifically
- [[NEW]]“You used the text to answer that question instead of guessing — that’s exactly what strong readers do.”[[/NEW]]
- [[NEW]]“You kept going after a hard question and finished the next one. That shows real work stamina.”[[/NEW]]
- [[NEW]]“Your written answer explained the author’s idea clearly and used evidence from the article. That’s grade-level thinking.”[[/NEW]]

## Part B — Modified Lesson Plan

### 1) Intro Slide Deck — 5 min
- Original step: Guide students through the slide deck to introduce them to the reading lesson and skill focus.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Keep Devon in whole group, but give him the goal for the lesson in one spoken sentence and one written sentence on the board: “Today we’re finding the central idea and details that support it.”[[/CHANGED]] Then hand him the reading organizer so he can track the central idea as you go.
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Goal 2 — Written expression — at grade level: “independently complete and submit grade-level written responses”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Present level: “Reading on grade level. Written expression is on grade level when not under time/performance pressure”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]1-line lesson target + central idea organizer with boxes for “main idea” and “details.”[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]At the start of the lesson, before the text is introduced.[[/NEW]]

### 2) Vocabulary / Purpose for Reading — embedded in whole class intro
- Original step: Introduce vocabulary and purpose for reading.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Show the four vocabulary words on the board and quietly hand Devon a mini-glossary with student-friendly meanings next to each word. Ask him to underline only the words he hears in the text instead of copying all definitions.[[/CHANGED]]
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Directions given in writing whenever oral directions are given”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Written response accepted in place of any oral response”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]Mini-glossary strip for aspect, moral, narrative, specific; underline-only task.[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]During the intro slide deck, before the reading begins.[[/NEW]]

### 3) Paragraphs 1–2 — Whole class reading + DRQ A/B/C
- Original step: Read paragraphs 1–2 and answer the during-reading questions.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Have Devon follow along while you read aloud, then stop and point to Paragraph 1. Ask him to answer DRQ A in writing on his organizer instead of speaking. For DRQ B, give him the sentence frame: “The bulleted list shows that a good definition of community must ____.” [[/CHANGED]] For DRQ C, let him choose 2 of the 4 key traits instead of all 3, then add a third if he’s ready.
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Accommodation — “No cold-calling — Devon raises hand or signals to participate”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Written response accepted in place of any oral response”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Goal 2 benchmark — “Use a written planning template (claim → 3 evidence → conclusion) before drafting, 4/5 assignments.”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]DRQ organizer with sentence stems and a 2-box evidence tracker.[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]Immediately after reading Paragraphs 1–2 and before the class moves on.[[/NEW]]

### 4) Paragraphs 3–7 — Partner reading + DRQ A/B
- Original step: Partner read and respond to the questions.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Keep him in the regular partner structure, but assign his partner a clear job: read the paragraph aloud while Devon tracks one detail on his organizer, then Devon writes the answer. If the partner reads too fast, pause and reread only the key sentence for Devon.[[/CHANGED]]
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Present level: “Strong written work when given choice of topic and quiet setting”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Goal 2 — “at grade level” written expression[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Directions given in writing whenever oral directions are given”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]One-question-at-a-time partner reading card and a detail finder box labeled “What does this paragraph show?”[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]During the partner-reading section, right after Paragraph 3 and again after Paragraphs 5–7.[[/NEW]]

### 5) Paragraph 8 — Whole class reading + DRQ A/B
- Original step: Read paragraph 8 and answer the questions.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Give Devon the paragraph 8 checklist before reading: “circle the second list, box one reason Lowe gives, star the sentence that restates the definition.” After reading, have him answer DRQ A by pointing to the second bulleted list and writing one sentence only. For DRQ B, offer a choice of two response starters.[[/CHANGED]]
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Goal 2 benchmark — “Respond in writing to teacher feedback within 48 hours, 4/5 instances.”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Errors not marked in red ink”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Present level: “strongly prefers writing over speaking”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]Paragraph 8 checklist + choice of 2 sentence starters.[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]At the paragraph 8 stop point, before moving to paragraph 9.[[/NEW]]

### 6) Paragraphs 9–11 — Whole class reading + evidence finding
- Original step: Read paragraphs 9–11 and highlight evidence that answers the title question.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Give Devon a two-column organizer labeled “What is community?” and “Why is it important?” and have him highlight only two evidence lines: one from paragraph 9 and one from paragraph 11. Then he writes one short sentence under each.[[/CHANGED]]
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]RI.7.2 lesson skill — “determining and summarizing the central idea of a text and identifying the details that develop it”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Goal 2 — written response at grade level[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]Two-column evidence organizer with highlight boxes for paragraphs 9 and 11.[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]Right after the whole-class reading of paragraphs 9–11.[[/NEW]]

### 7) Independent Practice — MCQs #1–4 (5 min)
- Original step: Students answer 4 multiple-choice questions.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Keep the same 4 questions, but give him the version with one-sentence glosses under each question and the answer choices spaced farther apart. Let him answer directly on the paper, and if he freezes, cover the page so he sees only one question at a time.[[/CHANGED]]
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Extra time (2x) on all assessments”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Seat near door for low-profile exit”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Assessment accommodation — “Separate or alternate test location — REQUIRED, not optional”[[/NEW]] (used for formal assessments, not this lesson’s practice questions); [[NEW]]Present level — “On grade level across content areas when work is completed.”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]Reduced-load MCQ page with glosses and a one-question cover strip.[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]As soon as the class moves into Independent Practice.[[/NEW]]

### 8) Independent Practice — short response prompt (15 min)
- Original step: Students answer the short response prompt.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Give Devon the same prompt, but add the sentence stems and checklist on the page. Tell him he only needs to draft first; you’ll check after the first 2 sentences. If he’s stuck, point him back to paragraph 9 and paragraph 11, not to a new explanation.[[/CHANGED]]
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Goal 2 benchmark — “Use a written planning template (claim → 3 evidence → conclusion) before drafting, 4/5 assignments.”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Typed response accepted for any handwritten task”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Option to submit work via shared doc rather than hand in physically”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]Short-response page with claim/evidence/conclusion stems and a 3-item checklist.[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]Right after the MCQs, during the written response portion.[[/NEW]]

### 9) Student-Led Discussion — 5 min
- Original step: Students discuss the questions with a partner.
- Modified step for Devon: [[CHANGED]]Keep Devon in the partner discussion, but let him answer question 1 and 3 in writing on the page instead of speaking, and let him choose one sentence to read aloud only if he volunteers. Do not call on him.[[/CHANGED]]
- IEP citation: [[NEW]]Accommodation — “No cold-calling — Devon raises hand or signals to participate”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Accommodation — “Written response accepted in place of any oral response”[[/NEW]]; [[NEW]]Goal 1 — Anxiety self-management: “deploy a taught coping strategy ... BEFORE escalation”[[/NEW]]
- Exact materials swap or scaffold: [[NEW]]Discussion page with written-response boxes only for Devon.[[/NEW]]
- When: [[NEW]]At the final discussion block, after independent work.[[/NEW]]

D. to give a clear and useful definition of community
